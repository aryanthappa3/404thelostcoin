ABOUT 404COIN

WHAT IS 404?

404Coin is a revolutionary memecoin built on Binance Smart Chain — born not from success, but from failure itself. Taking inspiration from the legendary 404 Error - Page Not Found, this coin symbolizes the unpredictable, chaotic, and often hilarious side of the internet.

In a world where coins chase hype, utilities, and roadmaps that never load, 404 embraces the void — it celebrates being lost, glitched, and absurd.

THE ORIGIN

404 was launched by an anonymous crew of degens, developers, and digital misfits who were tired of utility-laden whitepapers no one reads and Telegram groups filled with buzzwords. Instead, they asked:

"What if the coin itself is the error?"

And so, 404 was not found — it was created.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Just like the infamous error message, 404 is for those who never fit in, for whom the internet is home, and who believe that sometimes the biggest wins come from things not working at all.

So if you're tired of being serious, of chasing promises, and you believe the glitch is the feature — then welcome.
